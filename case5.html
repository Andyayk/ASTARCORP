Company Description

LionGold Corp Ltd is Singapore-based gold company involved in gold exploration, mine development and gold production. The Company's concessions are in Australia, Africa, and in North and South America. In June 2013, LionGold Corp Ltd announced that it has established two wholly owned subsidiaries, which include LIONGOLD South America Ltd and Liongold Bolivia Ltd. In July 2013, LionGold Corp Ltd announced the disposal of the property located at 38 Kallang Place, Singapore 339166. Effective September 27, 2013, LionGold Corp Ltd acquired a 17.465% interest in A1 Consolidated Gold Ltd, a gold mining company. In October 2013, Acadian Mining Corporation and LionGold Corp. Ltd announced that they concluded the arrangement by which the Company, through its wholly owned subsidiary LionGold Mining Canada Inc acquired Acadian Mining Corporation.
