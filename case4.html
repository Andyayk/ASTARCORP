Company Description

Dragon Group International Limited (Dragon Group) is a Singapore-based company engaged in investment holding and acts as corporate manager and advisor in relation to the administration and organisation of the businesses of its subsidiary companies.The Company is engaged in Electronics Distribution and Technology Investments and Others. Electronics Distribution ,which includes trading and distribution and acting as commission agents in electronic components and products, provision of freight and warehousing facilities, material and inventory management, delivery and invoicing using Web-based solutions and provision of semiconductor application in consumer electronics, computer peripheral and communication solutions.
